StarWars Phantom Menance Summary:
Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan travel to stop the Trade Federation.
Viceroy Gunray tries to kill the two Jedi under order of Darth Sidious
The Jedi flee to Naboo to take the Queen to safety, but are stranded on Tatooine.
The group meet young Anakin Skywalker, who they then free and take back to Coruscant.
The group try to ask for galactic aid, but is turned down.
The Jedi return to Naboo to fight the Trade Federation.
The group recruit the help of the Gungans to aid in the fight.
